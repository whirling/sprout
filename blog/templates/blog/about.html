sprout
 about

Sprout is a website that serves as a central hub of mental health resources for teens in NYC.
Sprout is the final project of Nowshin Sayara, Emma Logsdon, and Kyra Cousins, who are participating in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program hosted at the AT&T offices in New York City.
Sprout is made possible by our incredible teaching team, Lucie, Haotian, and Megan, by the invaluable resources on W3 Schools and Djangogirls, and by the support of Girls Who Code, AT&T, and viewers like you.
